girl in red is the stage name of Marie Ulven, a Norwegian singer, songwriter, and musician. Her music often explores themes of love, mental health, and self-discovery.

girl in red's music is characterized by its confessional and intimate nature, often drawing from her own experiences and emotions, as seen from her top hits "We Fell in Love in October" and "I Wanna Be Your Girlfriend". She has a knack for creating introspective and heartfelt songs that connect with her audience on an emotional level.

#indiepop #bedroompop

The 1975 is a British pop rock band formed in 2002, known for their eclectic sound and thought-provoking lyrics.

Their hits, including "Chocolate", "Somebody Else", and "Love It If We Made It" showcase their ability to craft infectious melodies surrounding a range of topics from love and heartbreak to social commentary. The band's versatile sound and willingness to experiment have solidified their status as one of the most influential and innovative acts of their generation.

#poprock #indierock #electropop
